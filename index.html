Як отримати бонус?

Football betting is the recommended option for beginners in the world of betting.

Initially, football betting was a form of entertainment. For example, we can recall the totalizator — an ancient pool system that emerged in Britain at the beginning of the 20th century with the following conditions:

  • Near stadiums, fans purchased tickets at a minimal price (later, tickets began to be distributed through newspapers, where they were cut out);
  • On cards, fans filled in the results of upcoming "game number one" matches that they considered most likely;
  • Coupons were handed to the pool collecting agent or sent by mail to the organizing company.

A specialized office guaranteed that there would be at least one winner per week. Winners were determined by the number of points scored among all fans who sent in their cards.

The totalizator system made it possible to earn large amounts of money by placing the first football bets. For example, Michael Elliott, risking only 2 pounds sterling, earned over 3 million pounds. He managed to correctly predict 8 matches that ended in a 2:2 draw.

The pool system was successful until "bookmakers" began to appear in the form they exist today. Fans of "game number one" gained the opportunity to place bets with much greater comfort.
